Explore the Beauty of Kashmir

Dal Lake

Dal Lake is the jewel of Srinagar, known for its beautiful houseboats and shikara rides. The reflection of the surrounding mountains in the calm waters is breathtaking.

Pahalgam

Pahalgam is a lush green valley surrounded by snow-capped peaks. It is a perfect spot for trekking, camping, and enjoying nature's serenity.

Shurunz Waterfall

Shurunz Waterfall is a hidden gem tucked away in the valleys of Kashmir. The peaceful environment and sound of the cascading water make it a must-visit.

Daksum

Daksum is a beautiful forested area ideal for nature lovers. It offers cool breeze, thick pine forests, and flowing streams perfect for relaxation.

Gulmarg

Gulmarg is a popular ski resort and home to one of the highest cable cars in the world. In winter, it's a snowy paradise, and in summer, a flowery meadow.

Sonmarg

Sonmarg, meaning 'Meadow of Gold', offers stunning views of glaciers and snow-covered mountains. It is a starting point for many trekking routes.
