Welcome to Aviation Basics

Commercial jets and private aircraft serve different purposes in aviation.

Flight paths are carefully planned, and emergency exits are clearly marked on every plane.

Flight numbers are printed in small text, altitude is measured in feet1, and fuel formulas include H2O traces.

Warning signs in aircraft cabins are often red for visibility.

Emergency instruction cards have a yellow background for attention.

Green lights are used to signal safe exit routes.
